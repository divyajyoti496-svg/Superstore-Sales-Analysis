# Superstore Sales Analysis


## Objective

Analyze retail sales data to identify trends, high-performing customer segments, and category-level performance to support business decision-making.

## Dataset

* Superstore Sales Dataset
* Includes order details, customer information, product categories, and revenue metrics

## Tools & Technologies

* Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
* Jupyter Notebook

## Key Analysis

* Data cleaning and preprocessing
* Feature engineering (Year, Month, Day extraction)
* Customer-level revenue analysis
* Category-wise performance evaluation

## Key Insights

* Technology category contributes the highest share of total revenue
* A small group of customers drives a disproportionate amount of sales
* Sales concentration indicates dependency on specific product segments
